About Me

Hi! I'm Harsha, a computer science student at Indian Institue of Technology Dhanbad(Indian School of Mines) with a keen interest in web development. I enjoy turning ideas into responsive and clean websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I'm currently diving into React and exploring backend development with Node.js. I'm also practicing algorithms on Codeforces and LeetCode to improve my problem-solving skills.

I aspire to become a full-stack developer and build applications that make a difference. Outside of coding, I love watching tech videos and tweaking my personal projects.
